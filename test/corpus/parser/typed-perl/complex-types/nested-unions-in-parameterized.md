# Nested Unions In Parameterized

```perl
my ArrayRef[Int|Str|Bool] @complex_array;
my HashRef[ArrayRef[Int]|HashRef[Str]] %nested_complex;
my Map[Str, Int|Undef] %optional_values;
```
